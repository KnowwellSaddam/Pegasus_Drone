Who We Are

Adjoining Curiosity & Innovation

Before starting our “About Us” section, we can quote anything that will give a better picture of the CEO’s core values.

Pegasus
“Innovation Starts From The Very Day When Your Brain Crafts An Idea So Unique That It’s Worth Taking Every Shot”
- Morgan Mikloi

Pegasus is continuously creating high performance, safe and autonomous drones that enhance productivity and security. Creating and inspiring cutting-edge solutions is in our DNA, and we make use of it by providing our customers with capabilities that were once deemed impossible. Our drones redefine engineering with camera stabilisation and intelligent image processing.

At Pegasus, we are encouraging fearless ideation through open culture and focusing on a deep understanding of fundamentals. Lastly, we understand our responsibility towards society and the generations to come. That's why we are a team of brilliant minds that embodies energy, vision, and eco-friendly technology.
